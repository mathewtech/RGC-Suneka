About Our Church

Redeemed Gospel Church exists to glorify God, serve people and spread the message of Jesus Christ in Kenya and beyond.

Our Mission

To love God, love people, and make disciples of all nations. We are committed to living out the Great Commission in all we do.

Our Vision

To build a Christ-centered community that brings transformation to lives through worship, outreach, and teaching of the Word.

Our Core Values

  • Faith: Trusting God in all things.
  • Compassion: Serving people with love and humility.
  • Integrity: Living according to biblical truth.
  • Community: Building strong relationships centered on Christ.

Our Story

Founded in the early 2000s, Redeemed Gospel Church Suneka began with a handful of believers gathering to pray. By God’s grace, we have grown into a vibrant community impacting many lives in Suneka and beyond through worship services, community outreach, and youth programs.
